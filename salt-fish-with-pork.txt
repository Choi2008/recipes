Ingredients:
 - 2 oz of salt fish
 - 3 oz of half fat half thin pork meat
 - 2 teaspoons baking soda
 - 1 teaspoon sugar
 - 1 teaspoon corn oil

Directions:
 1. Chop the pork meat into fine grains and put them in a bowl.
 
 2. Add 2 teaspoons baking soda, 2 teaspoons of water, and 1 teaspoon
    sugar into the pork .
 
 3. Grap the mixture and throw it back into the bowl repeatly until 
    it turns out into a paster.
	
 4. Put the paster into a dish.
 
 5. Add 1 piece of salt fish on the top.
 
 6. Add some gingers.
 
 6. Add 1 teaspoon corn oil over the mixture.
 
 7. Steam the dish for about 8 minutes.

 8. The dish is now ready to serve.
